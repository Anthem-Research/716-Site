Scope

716 is not positioned as an architecture practice. It is a practical project management and execution arm for work that benefits from clear thinking, good documentation, reliable coordination, and careful physical follow through.

Projects may include design input, sourcing, subcontractor coordination, repair work, maintenance planning, site documentation, and simple physical improvements.

Information

Based in Plettenberg Bay. Available for selected work in Plett, the Garden Route, and regional projects where the scope is coherent.
